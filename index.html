Whatever Restaurant is proud to play an active role in our community. One of the ways in which we do this is by supporting groups and charities that are important to our customers or that make a difference to the lives of Australians. At the heart of our commitment to giving back to the community is our many and various charities to Melbourne's Royal Children's Hospital - specifically the Good Friday Appeal. The Good Friday Appeal helps thousands of seriously ill children and their families every year.
